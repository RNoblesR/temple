Donation

Sik Sik Yuen is an approved tax exempt charitable organization. The main source of funding comes from generous donors and subsidies of the Government. Apart from supporting charity works such as social services, education, medical services, relief of sufferings, donations will also contribute to religious affairs and daily operation of Wong Tai Sin Temple.

Donation Method

Donation Amount

Donate with HK$ 100 or above could enjoy tax-waive from Government.

Cheque

2.Download and complete the donation form

3.Mail both cheque and donation form to us by post: Sik Sik Yuen, 2 Chuk Yuen Village, Wong Tai Sin, Kowloon.

4.Official receipt will be issued for donation.

* Donors with donation of HK$100 or above can apply for tax deduction with official receipt.
